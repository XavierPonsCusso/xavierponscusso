#Layout


This layout design was inspired by the vibrant party club DJ scene in Barcelona. The concept was to create a magazine that showcases the diverse and rich music culture of the city. To achieve this, I selected famous artist photos and used them to design a cover, an index, a miscellaneous page, a four-page interview article, and a short article.


While the content is fictitious and uses the classic placeholder text, Lorem Ipsum, the goal was to create a professional-looking publication that would entice readers to explore the music scene in Barcelona. Through this project, I was able to hone my skills in layout design and graphic design principles, including typography, color, and composition.
