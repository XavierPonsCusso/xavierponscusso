ESTROPAJO IS A PERSONAL PROJECT THAT FUSES MUSIC, ELECTRONICS, AND PRODUCT DESIGN.

DEVELOPED IN COLLABORATION WITH DESIGNER SARA LÓPEZ MORALES (PARAMETRIP.COM), THIS PROJECT AIMS TO EXPLORE THE CREATIVE POTENTIAL OF ARDUINO, BASIC ELECTRONIC COMPONENTS, AND MUSIC.

HOW

THE PROJECT IS CONTROLLED BY TWO POTENTIOMETERS THAT ADJUST THE SOUND WAVE, PRODUCING A UNIQUE AND CUSTOMIZABLE AUDIO EXPERIENCE. THE STEREO AUDIO JACK 3.5 OUTPUT DELIVERS A HIGH-QUALITY SOUND THAT IS PERFECT FOR USE WITH SPEAKERS.
WHAT MAKES ESTROPAJO STAND OUT IS ITS UNIQUE AND PLAYFUL DESIGN. BY INCORPORATING ALL OF THE ELECTRONIC COMPONENTS INSIDE A KITCHEN SCOURER, IT ADDS A HUMOROUS TOUCH TO THE PROJECT. THIS PROJECT DEMONSTRATES OUR PASSION FOR EXPLORING NEW POSSIBILITIES IN THE INTERSECTION OF TECHNOLOGY, DESIGN, AND MUSIC.
